Humble Beginnings

Pugsicles Inc. started out as a small, independent shoppe. There was only one pug at the time, and only one human. Though unknown at the time, the masterpieces they created were unlike anything else.

Their first design, "Fight for Food," artfully captured the restlessness of the modern day pug, and their desire to be freed from their shackles. It resonated with pugs all over the globe.

Their next piece, considered to be the soul of a generation, spread like wildfire. It was another great work, giving hope to the bleak struggle of everyday life... for pugs.

The Forgotten Pugs

To this day, there remains only a handful of pictures left. We scoured the globe searching for the last remnants of Pugsicles Inc., and of it's beloved model, Kimmie The Pug.
